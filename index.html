We offer a wide variety of entertainment at any occasion for all audiences!

Our aim is to present an unforgettable and festive encounter with the audience that will make any event unique, adding lift to make it a hit.
Based in Pietermaritzburg, we travel throughout KZN and also further afield throughout South Africa for large scale events.
We also have educational fun physics shows for schools, as well as team building for companies.
Here are a few of our more popular characters and shows:

Kimbo Comedy Magic

A high energy character with a strong foreign accent who performs mainly magic, combined with mime, juggling, balloon modelling and illusions to add a festive atmosphere to a venue or an occasion. Kimbo moves around the entire venue interacting with the crowd by using close up comedy magic and allowing them to “generate magic” themselves. This is aimed at both adults and/or children. Kimbo is also available to do table to table magic, busking, face painting, unicycling or other specific requests.

Lollipop

A delightful clownish rag doll, performing magic, balloon models, face painting, dance and activities.

Survivor

The RED TRIBE compete against the GREEN TRIBE for two hours of fast pace, short activities, testing their abilities of speed, skill, endurance, wit, fears, strength, courage and appetite. No voting out, highly bias scoring and loads of fun. Ideal for corporate team building. All ages.

Mr. Pepperochelli The Bullgician

An “over the top”, absent minded, animated, comedy magician who looks a bit like Albert Einstein. Ideal for adult formal functions that require entertainment integrated with Master of Ceremonies, judging, etc.


Magic Man

A colourful character that presents a variety of stage magic, close up magic, etc. Ideal for young and old alike. Private, public and corporate events.

Nikkitabell The Fairy

Nikkitabell the Fairy is a much loved authentic fairy. She presents close up magic on an individual basis as well as song stories, balloon modelling and face painting. For parties she can also conduct various activities for the children. A personalised poem to the Birthday child is also presented.

Army

Action Man (or Woman) conducts rigorous army / jungle survival theme activities.
Ages : 5 upwards.
